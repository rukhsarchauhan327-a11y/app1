Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add new products to inventory?

Go to Inventory → Add New Item. Fill in product details including name, price, and stock quantity.

How to generate bills for customers?

Use the Cart section to add items, select customer, and generate bill. You can print or share digitally.

How to track customer credit payments?

Customer ledger shows all credit transactions. You can add payments and track outstanding balances.

Can I export business data?

Yes, go to Profile → Export Data to download comprehensive business reports as PDF.

How to manage staff accounts?

Use the Staff section to add team members, set permissions, and track their activities.
